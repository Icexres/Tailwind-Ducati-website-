The Ducati Panigale V2

The new Ducati Panigale V2 marks a historic transition in the history of Borgo Panigale twin-cylinder sports bikes, because for the first time it is not derived from the larger motorcycle that competes in Superbike. Designed totally from scratch, it is the lightest ever Panigale. It weighs 17 kg less than the previous model, from which it inherits its sports attitude and adds a level of ease, comfort and riding pleasure.

ABOUT US
Ducati is a legendary Italian motorcycle manufacturer, renowned for its passion for performance, cutting-edge technology, and iconic design. Founded in 1926 in Bologna, Italy, Ducati has consistently pushed the boundaries of innovation, creating high-performance motorcycles that blend precision engineering with Italian craftsmanship. With a rich racing heritage in MotoGP, World Superbike, and other prestigious competitions, Ducati continues to lead the industry, delivering motorcycles that offer unmatched power, agility, and style. Dedicated to inspiring riders worldwide, Ducati combines tradition with forward-thinking, ensuring every ride is an exhilarating experience while embracing sustainability and future technologies for a new generation of riders.
